Welcome to henppy Taiwanese Snacks!

Welcome to our e-commerce site that sells delicious Taiwanese snacks. We are group JEM and our members are Jacky Yang, Emily Chou, and Morris Chen.
All three of us are from Taiwan and we all miss food from our home. We decided to use this project to promote these delicious Taiwanese snacks to everyone.

A little about our name! We decided on the name henppy ("hen-py") becuase it sounds like very naughty in mandarin. As taiwanese, we all love puns and word play, we thought this name would represent our site becuase we can just play around and have fun, be a little naughty.

We hope you enjoy our site! If you have any questions or would like to give us some suggestions, feel free to hit the Contact Us page and send us a message!
